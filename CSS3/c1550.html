Rare earth Nuggets is a technology content sharing community and a platform for discovering and sharing high-quality content based on Internet technology, Including daily selected content under the categories of IOS, Android, front end, back end and design. It mainly distributes the content of new technologies to the technical developers, product managers, operators and other people of Internet companies. The mobile terminal also has records of user behavior such as collection and reading, so that users can more easily find articles they have read or collected.
